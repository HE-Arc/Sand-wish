Welcome on Sand'wish!

Sand'wish regroups your wishlists for your events as well as for your friends. You can create a wishlist and add some personalized gifts. Your friends can then contribute partially or totally to the amount of gifts they choose. Once received you can mark a gift as validated so everyone knows you got it. Of course once received you will be able to see who contributed.

Start now!

You can naviguate this website without an account but won't be able to create wishlists or contribute to gifts. Sign up to start now or search a user by his username to see his wishlists.
